In this project, there are two major parties: the admin side and the client side. The admin users are medical practitioners. The client users are patients. The admin users and client users can register accounts by themselves.

Each patient needs to link with a practitioner to get prescription. To do so, the patient goes to their practitioner and tell them their user info, for example user id and their name. The practitioner on their side search for the user and connect with them. In this way, the practitioner can give this patient a prescription.

Once the prescription is given, it is sent to the patient's account. They can view their prescription on their end.
